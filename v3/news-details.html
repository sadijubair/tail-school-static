Annual Science Fair 2025 Showcases Student Innovation and Scientific Excellence

Our students presented groundbreaking projects in robotics, environmental science, and biotechnology, demonstrating exceptional creativity and scientific thinking.

SA

Dr. Sarah Ahmed

Academic Coordinator ‚Ä¢ Science Department

The Annual Science Fair 2025 at Govt. Hazi Mohammad Mohsin College proved to be a resounding success, showcasing the remarkable talents and scientific acumen of our students. Over 150 innovative projects were displayed across various scientific disciplines, demonstrating our commitment to fostering scientific inquiry and practical learning.

Groundbreaking Student Projects

This year's fair featured exceptional projects spanning multiple fields of science and technology. Students worked tirelessly over several months to conceptualize, research, and execute their innovative ideas under the guidance of our dedicated faculty members.

Highlight of the Event

The winning project, "AI-Powered Waste Segregation System," developed by students Fatima Rahman and Ahmed Hassan, impressed judges with its practical application and environmental impact potential. The system uses machine learning to identify and sort waste materials with 95% accuracy.

Industry Expert Panel

The fair was judged by a distinguished panel of scientists, engineers, and academicians from leading universities and research institutions. Their valuable feedback provided students with insights into real-world applications of their projects and potential career paths in scientific research.

Looking Forward

The success of this year's Science Fair reinforces our institution's commitment to promoting STEM education and practical learning. We are already planning next year's event with expanded categories and increased industry partnerships to provide even more opportunities for our students to showcase their talents.
