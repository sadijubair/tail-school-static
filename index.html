Excellence in Education Since 1874

Govt. Hazi Mohammad Mohsin College stands as one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions in the region. With over 150 years of academic excellence, we have been shaping minds and building futures for generations of students.

Our commitment to holistic education combines rigorous academics with character development, preparing students to become leaders and innovators in their chosen fields.
