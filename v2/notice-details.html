Examination Published: December 10, 2024

Annual Examination Schedule 2024 Published

Posted by: Administration Views: 1,248

Important Notice

All students must read this notice carefully and follow the instructions mentioned below.

Notice Description

This is to inform all the students of Class IX, X, XI, and XII that the Annual Examination Schedule for the academic year 2024 has been published. Students are requested to check the examination routine carefully and prepare accordingly.

The examination will commence from January 15, 2025 and will continue till February 10, 2025. All students must report to their respective examination halls at least 30 minutes before the examination starts.

Important Instructions:

  • Students must bring their admit card and ID card to the examination hall
  • Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited
  • Students should arrive at least 30 minutes before the exam starts
  • Late entry after 15 minutes of exam start will not be allowed
  • Students must maintain exam hall discipline

Examination Centers:

  • Center 1: Main College Building - Class IX & X
  • Center 2: Science Building - Class XI Science
  • Center 3: Arts Building - Class XI & XII Arts
  • Center 4: Commerce Building - Class XI & XII Commerce

Contact Information:

For any queries regarding the examination, students can contact the examination committee:

  • +880 123 456 7890
  • exam@ghmmc.edu.bd
  • Office Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
